Word 4

Definition: "Loquacious" describes someone who is very talkative or chatty, often to the point of excess.

Uses:

  • While being loquacious can be an asset in certain professions, it's important to balance talking with active listening.
  • During the meeting, the loquacious team member dominated the conversation, leaving little room for others to express their ideas.
  • Her loquaciousness became evident during the interview, as she effortlessly shared stories and insights about her experiences.
